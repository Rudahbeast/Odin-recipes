How To Make Beans

Make sure your beans is clean.

Rinse with water then turn into a pot with adequate water

switch on your gas then let the beans boil (might take some time) :)

Make your sauce by blending onions,pepper and lttle tomatoes (tomatoes is optional) Fry the sauce with red oil

Add your Seasonings,then allow to fry.add the sauce to the beans when th beans is soft enough,then stir

INGREDIENTS

  • Beans
  • Read oil
  • Onions and pepper
  • Seasonings

STEPS

  1. Boil the beans
  2. blend the onions and pepper
  3. Fry the blended onion and pepper
  4. Mix sauce with beans
