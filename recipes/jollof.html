How To Make Jollof Rice

First parboil your white Rice,blend your tomatoes

Then make your tomatoes sauce,add water to the fried sauce

pour the rice into the diluted sauce,cover and let cook

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper and Onions
  • seasoning and Salt

STEPS

  1. Parboiling
  2. Blending
  3. Seasoning
  4. Saucing and Frying
  5. Mix the Rice and Sauce
  6. Then let boil until soft enough for eating
