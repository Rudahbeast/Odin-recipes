How To Make Stew

Blend your tomatoes,pepper and stew

Add water into the already hot pot, then pour in your blended tomatoes allow the tomatoes to start boiling

Then add all your seasoning and soup condiments, then let fry till most of the water has evaporated and you can mainly see oil

INGREDIENTS

  • Tomatoes and pepper
  • Onions
  • Ground nut or Palm oil
  • Seasonings
  • Fish or Meat water (optional)

STEPS

  1. Blend your Tomatoes and Pepper
  2. Allow to steam
  3. Add your spices and Seasonings
  4. Then allow to fry
